# 📢 Ocoya — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Ocoya?

Ocoya is an **AI-powered social media automation and content creation platform**.  
It helps businesses, marketers, and creators **design posts, generate captions, schedule content, and analyze performance** across multiple platforms.  

Ocoya is widely used by **startups, agencies, and e-commerce brands** to save time and boost engagement.

---

## 🎯 2. What Can Ocoya Do? (Core Functions)

### ✍️ AI Copywriting
- Generate captions, hashtags, and ad copy  
- Multilingual support for global campaigns  
- Tailored messaging for different platforms  

### 🎨 Content Design
- Create graphics and visuals with templates  
- Integrate with Canva for design flexibility  
- Add product images for e-commerce  

### 📅 Social Media Scheduling
- Schedule posts across Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, TikTok, and more  
- Manage multiple accounts from one dashboard  
- Automate posting with smart scheduling  

### 📊 Analytics & Insights
- Track engagement and performance metrics  
- Optimize posting times  
- Identify top-performing content  

---

## ⭐ 3. Unique Features of Ocoya

- **Travis AI:** Built-in AI copywriter for captions and ads  
- **All-in-one dashboard:** Design, write, schedule, and analyze in one place  
- **E-commerce integration:** Sync with Shopify and WooCommerce  
- **Hashtag & emoji suggestions** for better reach  
- **Team collaboration tools**
