# ✍️ Peppertype AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Peppertype AI?

Peppertype AI is an **AI-powered copywriting and content generation platform**.  
It helps businesses, marketers, and creators produce **ad copy, blog posts, product descriptions, social media captions, and email content** quickly and at scale.  

Peppertype is widely used by **startups, agencies, and enterprises** to save time and maintain brand consistency.

## 🎯 2. What Can Peppertype AI Do? (Core Functions)

### 📢 Marketing Copy
- Generate ad copy for Google Ads, Facebook, and Instagram  
- Create headlines, slogans, and taglines  
- Write landing page content  

### 🛒 E-commerce Content
- Product descriptions for Shopify, Amazon, and WooCommerce
