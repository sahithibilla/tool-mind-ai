# ✍️ Hypotenuse AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Hypotenuse AI?

Hypotenuse AI is an **AI-powered content writing and marketing platform**.  
It helps businesses, marketers, and e-commerce brands generate **blogs, product descriptions, social media posts, and ad copy** at scale.  

Hypotenuse is widely used by **agencies, startups, and online retailers** to save time and maintain brand consistency.
