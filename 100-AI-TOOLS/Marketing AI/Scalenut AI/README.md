# 🚀 Scalenut AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Scalenut AI?

Scalenut AI is an **AI-powered SEO and content marketing platform** that helps businesses plan, research, and write content that ranks.  
It combines **AI writing assistance, keyword research, competitor analysis, and SEO optimization** into one workflow.  

Scalenut is widely used by **marketers, agencies, and enterprises** to scale content production and improve organic traffic.

---

## 🎯 2. What Can Scalenut AI Do? (Core Functions)

### ✍️ AI Writing Assistant
- Generate SEO-optimized blog posts, articles, and landing pages  
- Create outlines based on SERP data  
- Draft long-form content with AI  

### 🔍 Keyword Research
- Identify profitable keywords  
- Understand search intent  
- Build topic clusters  

### 📊 SERP Analyzer
- Compare content against top-ranking pages  
- Spot keyword gaps and content opportunities  
- Analyze headings, word counts, and backlinks  

### 🛠️ SEO Hub
- End-to-end workflow for keyword research, content planning, and optimization  
- Content scoring system for SEO readiness  

### 🤖 Cruise Mode
- One-click blog generation with AI + SEO insights
