# 🔄 LoopCV — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is LoopCV?

LoopCV is an **AI-powered job search automation platform** that automatically finds and applies to jobs on your behalf.  
You upload your CV, set preferences (job titles, locations, industries), and LoopCV mass-applies daily, even contacting recruiters directly.  
It’s designed to save time and increase interview opportunities.

---

## 🎯 2. What Can LoopCV Do? (Core Functions)

### ⚡ Mass Apply Daily
- Automatically applies to hundreds of jobs every day  
- Saves hours of manual searching and applying  

### 📝 Personalized Applications
- AI customizes each application to match job descriptions  
- Tailors CVs and cover letters for better recruiter visibility  

### 📧 Recruiter Outreach
- Finds recruiter emails and sends targeted messages  
- Expands reach beyond job boards  

### 📊 Dashboard & Tracking
- Monitor applications, interviews, and outcomes  
- Visualize job search progress
