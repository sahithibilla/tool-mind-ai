# 📄 Huntr AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Huntr AI?

Huntr AI is an **AI-powered job search management platform** that helps job seekers organize applications, track progress, and optimize resumes.  
It combines a **job tracker, resume builder, and career insights** into one tool, making the job search process more structured and efficient.

---

## 🎯 2. What Can Huntr AI Do? (Core Functions)

### 📊 Job Tracker
- Save and organize jobs from any job board using a browser extension  
- Track application status, deadlines, and recruiter contacts  
- Visualize job search progress with dashboards  

### 📝 AI Resume Builder
- Generate ATS-friendly resumes tailored to job descriptions  
- Provide keyword suggestions for better recruiter visibility  
- Offer customizable templates  

### 📑 Cover Letter Generator
- Create personalized cover letters aligned with resumes  
- Ensure consistent branding across applications  

### 🔍 Career Insights
- Analyze job postings for required skills  
- Recommend resume improvements for better match  
- Provide interview prep resources  

---

## ⭐ 3. Unique Features of Huntr AI

- **Centralized job search management** with a visual tracker  
- **AI-driven resume and cover letter optimization**  
- **Browser extension** for saving jobs directly from listings  
- **Progress dashboards** to monitor applications

## 💡 4. Main Use Cases

### 🎓 Students & Freshers

### 👩‍💼 Professionals

### 🔄 Career Switchers

## 💰 5. Pricing

- **Free Plan:** Basic job tracker and limited resume features  
- **Premium Plan (~$20/month):** Unlimited resumes, cover letters, advanced AI insights, and full job tracking features  
- **Team/Enterprise Plans:** Custom pricing for career services and recruiters
