# 📄 Teal AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Teal AI?

Teal AI is an **AI-powered career growth platform** that helps job seekers create ATS-friendly resumes, track job applications, and prepare for interviews.  
It combines a **resume builder, job tracker, cover letter generator, and interview assistant** into one centralized tool.

---

## 🎯 2. What Can Teal AI Do? (Core Functions)

### 📝 AI Resume Builder
- Generates ATS-optimized resumes tailored to job descriptions  
- Provides keyword suggestions for better recruiter visibility  
- Offers customizable templates  

### 📑 Cover Letter Generator
- Creates personalized cover letters aligned with resumes  
- Ensures consistent branding across applications  

### 📊 Job Tracker
- Chrome extension to bookmark jobs from any job board  
- Organize applications in one dashboard  
- Track deadlines, statuses, and recruiter contacts  

### 🎤 Interview Assistant
- Practice interview questions with AI feedback  
- Improve confidence and readiness  

### 🔍 Job Insights
- Analyze job postings for required skills  
- Recommend resume improvements for better match  

---

## ⭐ 3. Unique Features of Teal AI

- **Unlimited customized resumes** for every job application  
- **Centralized job search management** with the tracker  
- **AI-driven keyword optimization** for ATS compliance  
- **Beginner-friendly interface** with guided steps

## 💡 4. Main Use Cases
