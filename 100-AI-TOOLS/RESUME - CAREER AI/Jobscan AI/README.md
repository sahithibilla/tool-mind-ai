# 📄 Jobscan AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Jobscan AI?

Jobscan AI is an **AI-powered resume optimization platform** that helps job seekers tailor their resumes and cover letters to specific job descriptions.  
It’s designed to improve **ATS (Applicant Tracking System) compliance** and increase interview chances by analyzing keyword matches and skill alignment.

---

## 🎯 2. What Can Jobscan AI Do? (Core Functions)

### 📝 Resume Scanner
- Compares your resume against a job description  
- Provides a match rate score (keywords, skills, formatting)  
- Suggests improvements for ATS compliance  

### 📑 Cover Letter Optimization
- Tailors cover letters to job postings  
- Ensures consistent branding with resumes  

### 🔍 LinkedIn Optimization
- Analyzes LinkedIn profiles for recruiter visibility  
- Suggests keyword improvements  

### 📊 Jobscan Dashboard
- Track multiple resumes and job applications  
- Monitor match scores and progress  

### 🎤 Interview Prep
- Offers practice questions and AI feedback  
- Helps align answers with job requirements  

---

## ⭐ 3. Unique Features of Jobscan AI

- **Resume vs Job Description Match Rate** (percentage score)  
- **ATS-focused optimization** for resumes and cover letters  
- **LinkedIn profile analysis** for recruiter visibility  
- **Keyword targeting** based on job postings
