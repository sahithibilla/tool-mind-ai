# 📄 Rezi AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Rezi AI?

Rezi AI is an **AI-powered resume builder** that specializes in creating **ATS-optimized resumes**.  
It helps job seekers generate resumes, cover letters, and LinkedIn summaries that are tailored to specific job descriptions, ensuring higher chances of passing applicant tracking systems.

---

## 🎯 2. What Can Rezi AI Do? (Core Functions)

### 📝 AI Resume Builder
- Generates resumes optimized for ATS systems  
- Tailors resumes to specific job descriptions  
- Provides keyword suggestions for better recruiter visibility  

### 📑 Cover Letter Generator
- Creates personalized cover letters with AI assistance  
- Matches tone and style to resume  

### 🔍 Job Description Targeting
- Analyze job postings and highlight required skills  
- Suggests improvements to align resumes with job requirements  

### 🌐 LinkedIn Profile Optimization
- Generates professional LinkedIn summaries  
- Ensures consistency with resume content  

### 📊 Resume Scoring
- Provides feedback on resume strength  
- Rates resumes based on ATS compliance and keyword usage
