# 📄 Resume.io AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Resume.io AI?

Resume.io AI is an **AI-powered resume builder** that helps job seekers create professional, ATS-friendly resumes and cover letters.  
It provides **step-by-step guidance, recruiter matching, and interview prep tools**, making it easier to stand out in competitive job markets.

---

## 🎯 2. What Can Resume.io AI Do? (Core Functions)

### 📝 AI Resume Builder
- Automatically formats resumes for ATS compliance  
- Suggests improvements in grammar, structure, and keywords  
- Offers industry-specific templates  

### 📑 Cover Letter Builder
- Generates tailored cover letters based on job descriptions  
- Ensures consistent branding with resume design  

### 🔍 Recruiter Match
- Matches your resume with up to 50 recruiters weekly  
- Increases visibility in job markets  

### 💼 Job Board Integration
- Aggregates listings from multiple platforms  
- Allows direct applications from Resume.io dashboard  

### 🎤 Interview Prep
- Provides practice questions with instant AI feedback  
- Helps improve confidence and readiness
