# 📄 ResumeWorded AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is ResumeWorded AI?

ResumeWorded AI is an **AI-powered career platform** that helps job seekers improve resumes, LinkedIn profiles, and cover letters.  
It provides **instant feedback, keyword optimization, and recruiter insights** to boost interview chances and job search success.

---

## 🎯 2. What Can ResumeWorded AI Do? (Core Functions)

### 📝 Resume Optimization
- Analyzes resumes for ATS compliance  
- Provides keyword suggestions based on job descriptions  
- Scores resumes with actionable feedback  

### 📑 Cover Letter Review
- Evaluates cover letters for clarity and impact  
- Suggests improvements in tone and structure  

### 🔍 LinkedIn Optimization
- Reviews LinkedIn profiles for recruiter visibility  
- Suggests keyword and headline improvements  
- Provides a profile score with detailed feedback  

### 📊 Career Dashboard
- Track resume scores and LinkedIn improvements  
- Monitor progress over time  

### 🎤 Interview Prep (via insights)
- Offers guidance on aligning skills with job postings  
- Helps tailor responses to recruiter expectations  

---

## ⭐ 3. Unique Features of ResumeWorded AI

- **Resume Score & LinkedIn Score** for instant evaluation  
- **Keyword targeting** aligned with job postings  
- **Recruiter visibility insights** for LinkedIn optimization  
- **Actionable feedback** instead of generic suggestions

## 💡 4. Main Use Cases

### 🎓 Students & Freshers

### 👩‍💼 Professionals
