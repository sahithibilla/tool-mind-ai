# 🎬 InVideo AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is InVideo AI?

InVideo AI is an AI-powered video creation platform that allows users to generate complete videos from simple text prompts.

Users can describe the type of video they want, and InVideo AI automatically creates scripts, visuals, voiceovers, and scenes.

It is widely used by marketers, YouTubers, and businesses to create professional videos quickly without editing skills.
