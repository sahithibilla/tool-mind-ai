# 🎬 Pictory AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Pictory AI?

Pictory AI is an AI-powered video creation platform that allows users to generate videos automatically from text, articles, or scripts.

It can convert blog posts, articles, or long-form text into engaging videos with visuals, captions, and voiceovers.

Pictory is widely used by marketers, educators, and content creators to create videos quickly without editing skills.
