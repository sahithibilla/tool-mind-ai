# 🎬 Luma AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Luma AI?

Luma AI is an AI-powered platform that allows users to generate realistic videos and 3D scenes using artificial intelligence.

It is known for its **Dream Machine**, a powerful AI model that can generate videos from text prompts or images.

Luma AI is widely used by filmmakers, designers, and content creators to create cinematic AI-generated videos and 3D visualizations.
