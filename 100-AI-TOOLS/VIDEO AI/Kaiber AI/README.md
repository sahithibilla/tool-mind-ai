# 🎬 Kaiber AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Kaiber AI?

Kaiber AI is an AI-powered video generation platform that creates animated videos and visual effects from text prompts, images, or audio.

It is especially popular for creating AI-generated music videos, animated visuals, and artistic video content.

Kaiber is widely used by musicians, artists, and content creators to produce visually appealing AI videos.
