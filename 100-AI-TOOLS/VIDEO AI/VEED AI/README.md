# 🎬 VEED AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is VEED AI?

VEED AI is an online AI-powered video creation and editing platform that allows users to record, edit, and generate videos directly in a web browser.

It combines traditional video editing tools with AI features such as automatic subtitles, text-to-video generation, voice dubbing, and AI avatars.

The platform is widely used by content creators, businesses, and marketers to create professional videos quickly without complex editing software.
