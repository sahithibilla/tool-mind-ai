# 🎬 HeyGen — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is HeyGen?

HeyGen is an AI video generation platform that allows users to create videos using AI avatars and voice narration.

Users can simply type a script, and HeyGen generates a video where a digital avatar speaks the script naturally.

It is widely used for marketing videos, training materials, presentations, and educational content.

---

## 🎯 2. What Can HeyGen Do? (Core Functions)

### 🎥 AI Avatar Videos
- Create videos with AI presenters
- Generate talking avatar videos
- Produce presentation-style videos

### 📝 Text-to-Video
- Convert text scripts into videos
- Generate automatic voice narration
- Produce video scenes automatically

### 🌍 Multilingual Video Creation
- Generate videos in multiple languages
- Translate scripts automatically
- Create international marketing content

### 🎨 Video Customization
- Choose different avatars
- Customize backgrounds and layouts
- Adjust voice styles and tones
