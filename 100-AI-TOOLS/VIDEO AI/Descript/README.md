# 🎬 Descript — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Descript?

Descript is an AI-powered audio and video editing platform that allows users to edit videos and podcasts by editing text transcripts.

Instead of traditional timeline editing, users can edit video or audio simply by editing the transcript of the recording.

Descript is widely used by podcasters, YouTubers, and content creators to edit media quickly and efficiently.

---

## 🎯 2. What Can Descript Do? (Core Functions)

### 🎥 Video Editing
- Edit videos by editing text transcripts
- Cut or rearrange scenes easily
- Remove filler words automatically

### 🎙️ Podcast Editing
- Edit podcast recordings
- Clean audio automatically
- Add sound effects and music

### 🧠 AI Voice Generation (Overdub)
- Clone voices using AI
- Generate voiceovers automatically
- Fix spoken mistakes with AI voice replacement

### ✂️ Screen Recording
- Record screen and webcam videos
- Create tutorials and presentations

## ⭐ 3. Unique Features of Descript
