# 🎙️ Descript Overdub — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Descript Overdub?

Descript Overdub is an **AI-powered voice cloning and text-to-speech tool** built into the Descript audio/video editing platform.  
It allows users to create a **digital voice clone** that can generate speech from text, enabling seamless audio editing and narration.  

Overdub is widely used by **podcasters, video creators, educators, and businesses** for correcting recordings, generating narration, and producing content without re-recording.

---

## 🎯 2. What Can Descript Overdub Do? (Core Functions)

### 🗣️ Voice Cloning
- Create a digital clone of your voice  
- Generate speech from text input  
- Personalize narration with your own tone  

### 🎧 Text-to-Speech
- Convert scripts into natural audio  
- Choose from stock AI voices  
- Adjust pitch, speed, and style  

### 🎬 Audio/Video Editing Integration
- Replace mistakes in recordings without re-recording  
- Sync generated audio with video timelines  
- Edit podcasts and videos faster  

### 📚 Content Creation
- Narrate videos, podcasts, and e-learning content  
- Generate professional voiceovers  
- Automate repetitive audio tasks  

---

## ⭐ 3. Unique Features of Descript Overdub

### 🔹 Voice Cloning with Consent  
Users can create a voice clone only with explicit consent and samples.  

### 🔹 Seamless Editing  
Fix mistakes in audio by typing corrections — Overdub regenerates the voice.  

### 🔹 Stock AI Voices  
Access a library of pre-built voices for narration.  

### 🔹 Integrated with Descript  
Works directly inside Descript’s audio/video editor.  

### 🔹 Ethical Safeguards  
Voice cloning requires user permission to prevent misuse.
