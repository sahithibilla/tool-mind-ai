# 🎧 Speechify — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Speechify?

Speechify is an **AI-powered text-to-speech platform** that converts written text into natural-sounding audio.  
It helps users listen to books, articles, PDFs, and documents instead of reading them, making learning and productivity more accessible.  

Speechify is widely used by **students, professionals, and people with reading challenges (like dyslexia)** to consume content faster and more effectively.

---

## 🎯 2. What Can Speechify Do? (Core Functions)

### 🗣️ Text-to-Speech Conversion
- Convert text, PDFs, and articles into audio  
- Choose from multiple voices and accents  
- Adjust speed and tone  

### 🎧 Voice Customization
- Natural-sounding AI voices  
- Celebrity voice packs (premium)  
- Emotional tone adjustments  

### 📚 Content Accessibility
- Read aloud for dyslexia and ADHD support  
- Listen to study material and research papers  
- Improve comprehension and retention  

### 📱 Cross-Platform Support
- Works on iOS, Android, Chrome, and desktop  
- Sync across devices  
- Offline listening available  

---

## ⭐ 3. Unique Features of Speechify

### 🔹 Large Voice Library  
Wide range of realistic voices, including celebrity packs.  

### 🔹 Speed Control  
Listen at up to 9x speed for faster learning.  

### 🔹 OCR Support  
Scan physical books and convert them into audio.  

### 🔹 Cross-Platform Sync  
Seamlessly switch between devices.  

### 🔹 Accessibility Focus  
Designed to support dyslexia, ADHD, and other reading challenges.

## 🏢 6. Who Created Speechify?

- Founder: **Cliff Weitzman**  
- Company: Speechify Inc.  
- Country: USA  
- Launch Year: 2017  

Speechify was created to make reading accessible for everyone, especially those with dyslexia.
