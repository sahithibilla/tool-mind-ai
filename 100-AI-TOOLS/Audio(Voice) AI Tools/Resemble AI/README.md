# 🎙️ Resemble AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Resemble AI?

Resemble AI is an **AI-powered voice generation and cloning platform**.  
It enables users to create **realistic synthetic voices** for applications such as gaming, film, customer support, and accessibility.  

Resemble AI is widely used by **developers, content creators, and enterprises** to build custom voice experiences and integrate them into products.
