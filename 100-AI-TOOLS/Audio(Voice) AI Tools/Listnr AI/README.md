# 🎧 Listnr AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Listnr AI?

Listnr AI is an **AI-powered text-to-speech and audio publishing platform**.  
It allows users to convert written content into **realistic voiceovers** and publish them as podcasts or audio articles.  

Listnr is widely used by **bloggers, marketers, educators, and businesses** to repurpose written content into engaging audio formats.

## 🎯 2. What Can Listnr AI Do? (Core Functions)

### 🗣️ Text-to-Speech Conversion
- Convert blog posts, articles, and scripts into audio  
- Choose from 900+ voices across languages and accents  
- Adjust speed, pitch, and tone  

### 🎧 Podcast Creation
