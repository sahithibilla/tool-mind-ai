# 🎤 Cleanvoice AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Cleanvoice AI?

Cleanvoice AI is an **AI-powered audio editing tool** designed to automatically remove filler words, mouth sounds, stuttering, and background noise from recordings.  
It helps podcasters, content creators, and professionals produce **clean, polished audio** without manual editing.  

Cleanvoice is widely used for **podcasts, interviews, audiobooks, and professional recordings**.

---

## 🎯 2. What Can Cleanvoice AI Do? (Core Functions)

### 🗣️ Audio Cleanup
- Remove filler words (um, uh, like, etc.)  
- Eliminate mouth sounds and stuttering  
- Detect and cut dead air  

### 🎧 Noise Reduction
- Reduce background noise  
- Improve audio clarity  
- Enhance overall sound quality  

### 📚 Multilingual Support
- Works across multiple languages  
- Detects filler words in different dialects  

### 🛠️ Productivity Boost
- Save hours of manual editing  
- Export clean audio quickly  
- Integrate with podcast workflows
