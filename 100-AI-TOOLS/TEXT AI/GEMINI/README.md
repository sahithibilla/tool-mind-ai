# 🤖 Gemini — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Gemini?

Gemini is an AI assistant developed by Google that can understand and generate human-like text.

It is designed to help users with:

- Writing content
- Answering questions
- Coding assistance
- Research and learning

Gemini is part of Google's AI ecosystem and is integrated with many Google services.

### ✍️ Text Generation

### 🔎 Research Assistance

## ⭐ 3. Unique Features of Gemini

### 🔹 Google Ecosystem Integration
