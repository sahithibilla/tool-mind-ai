# ✍️ Grammarly AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Grammarly AI?

Grammarly AI is an AI-powered writing assistant that helps users improve grammar, spelling, clarity, and tone in their writing.

It is widely used by students, professionals, and businesses to produce clear and professional communication.

Grammarly works across many platforms including browsers, email, and document editors.

---

## 🎯 2. What Can Grammarly AI Do? (Core Functions)

### ✏️ Grammar Correction
- Detect grammar mistakes
- Suggest corrections
- Improve sentence structure

### 📝 Spelling Check
- Identify spelling errors
- Provide correct word suggestions

### 🎨 Tone Detection
- Detect writing tone
- Suggest improvements for professionalism or friendliness

### 📄 Writing Improvement
- Improve clarity
- Make sentences concise
- Enhance readability

### 🤖 AI Writing Assistance
- Generate text
- Rewrite sentences
- Help draft emails and documents
