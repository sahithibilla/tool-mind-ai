# 🧠 Notion AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Notion AI?

Notion AI is an AI-powered assistant built inside the Notion workspace that helps users write, summarize, organize information, and automate tasks.

It works directly within notes, documents, and databases, allowing users to generate content and manage knowledge without switching between tools. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}
