# ✍️ Copy.ai — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Copy.ai?

Copy.ai is an AI-powered writing assistant designed mainly for marketing and business content creation.

It helps users generate high-quality text quickly for things like advertisements, social media posts, emails, and product descriptions.

The tool is widely used by marketers, entrepreneurs, and content creators to automate writing tasks.
