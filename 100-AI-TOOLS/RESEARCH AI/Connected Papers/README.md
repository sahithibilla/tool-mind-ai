# 🧠 Connected Papers — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Connected Papers?

Connected Papers is an AI-powered research discovery tool that helps users explore academic papers through visual graphs.

It allows researchers to discover related research papers by analyzing citation relationships and similarities between studies.

Connected Papers is widely used by students, researchers, and academics to perform literature reviews and explore research topics.

---

## 🎯 2. What Can Connected Papers Do? (Core Functions)

### 🔎 Research Paper Discovery
- Discover related research papers
- Explore academic literature
- Find influential research studies

### 📊 Visual Research Graphs
- Display connections between research papers
- Show relationships between studies

### 📚 Literature Exploration
- Identify important papers in a field
- Explore research evolution over time

### 🧠 Citation Analysis
- Analyze how papers cite each other
- Discover foundational research papers

---

## ⭐ 3. Unique Features of Connected Papers

### 🔹 Research Graph Visualization
Shows research papers as nodes in a graph, connected by similarity.

### 🔹 Similar Paper Discovery
Find papers related to a specific study.

### 🔹 Prior and Derivative Works
Shows earlier and later research related to a paper.

### 🔹 Easy Literature Exploration
Helps researchers quickly explore a research field.
