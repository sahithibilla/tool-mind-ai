# 📊 Scite AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Scite AI?

Scite AI is an AI-powered research tool that helps users understand how scientific papers are cited by other research papers.

Unlike traditional citation tools, Scite shows whether a citation **supports, contradicts, or merely mentions** a research claim.

Scite AI is widely used by researchers, students, and academics to verify scientific evidence and evaluate research credibility.

## 🎯 2. What Can Scite AI Do? (Core Functions)

### 📄 Citation Analysis
- Analyze how research papers cite each other
- Identify supporting or contradicting citations

### 🔎 Research Paper Search
- Discover academic papers
- Explore research topics

### 📊 Smart Citations
- Show context of citations
- Indicate whether a paper supports or disputes a claim

### 🧠 Evidence Evaluation
- Assess research credibility
- Identify influential studies

## ⭐ 3. Unique Features of Scite AI

### 🔹 Smart Citations
Shows whether a research paper **supports, contradicts, or mentions** a claim.

### 🔹 Citation Context
