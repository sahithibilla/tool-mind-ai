# 📑 Scholarcy — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Scholarcy?

Scholarcy is an AI-powered research summarization tool that helps users quickly understand academic papers, reports, and research articles.

It automatically extracts key points, summaries, and important findings from research documents.

Scholarcy is widely used by students, researchers, and professionals who need to review large numbers of academic papers efficiently.

---

## 🎯 2. What Can Scholarcy Do? (Core Functions)

### 📄 Research Paper Summarization
- Generate summaries of academic papers
- Extract key points and findings
- Highlight important sections

### 📊 Flashcard Summaries
- Convert research papers into flashcards
- Show key concepts and results quickly

### 📚 Reference Extraction
- Extract references and citations
- Identify important sources

### 🧠 Knowledge Extraction
- Identify research methods
- Extract study results and conclusions
