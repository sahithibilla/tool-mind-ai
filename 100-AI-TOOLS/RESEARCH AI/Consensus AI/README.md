# 🔬 Consensus AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Consensus AI?

Consensus AI is an AI-powered academic search engine that helps users find answers directly from scientific research papers.

Instead of browsing many papers manually, Consensus AI analyzes research studies and summarizes scientific findings.

It is widely used by researchers, students, journalists, and professionals to find evidence-based answers.

---

## 🎯 2. What Can Consensus AI Do? (Core Functions)

### 🔎 Scientific Paper Search
- Search across thousands of research papers
- Find relevant academic studies
- Discover scientific evidence

### 📄 Research Summaries
- Summarize scientific papers
- Extract key conclusions
- Provide quick research insights

### 🧠 Evidence-Based Answers
- Provide answers backed by research studies
- Show supporting scientific papers

### 📊 Research Insights
- Identify research trends
- Analyze scientific findings

## ⭐ 3. Unique Features of Consensus AI

### 🔹 Evidence-Based Search
