# 📚 Elicit AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Elicit AI?

Elicit AI is an AI-powered research assistant designed to help users find and analyze academic research papers.

It uses AI to search research databases, summarize papers, and extract important insights from scientific studies.

Elicit AI is widely used by researchers, students, and analysts to conduct literature reviews and academic research efficiently.

---

## 🎯 2. What Can Elicit AI Do? (Core Functions)

### 🔎 Research Paper Search
- Find relevant academic papers
- Search research databases
- Discover new studies on specific topics

### 📄 Paper Summarization
- Summarize research papers
- Extract key insights
- Highlight important findings

### 📊 Data Extraction
- Extract information from research papers
- Identify study results and methodologies

### 🧠 Literature Review Assistance
- Compare multiple research papers
- Organize research findings
- Support academic research workflows
