# 🐇 Research Rabbit — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Research Rabbit?

Research Rabbit is an AI-powered academic discovery platform that helps researchers find relevant research papers and track research trends.

It works like a "Spotify for research papers" by recommending papers based on the topics and papers you follow.

Research Rabbit is widely used by students, academics, and researchers to explore scientific literature and discover related research.
