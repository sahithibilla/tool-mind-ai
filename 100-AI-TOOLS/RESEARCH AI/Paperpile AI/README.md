# 📚 Paperpile AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Paperpile AI?

Paperpile AI is a research reference management tool that helps users organize academic papers, manage citations, and create bibliographies.

It integrates with Google Docs and academic databases to make writing research papers easier.

Paperpile is widely used by students, researchers, and academics to manage research libraries and references efficiently.

---

## 🎯 2. What Can Paperpile AI Do? (Core Functions)

### 📄 Reference Management
- Organize research papers
- Create a personal research library
- Store PDFs and documents

### 📚 Citation Generation
- Generate citations automatically
- Create bibliographies in different formats
- Insert citations directly into documents

### 🔎 Research Paper Import
- Import papers from Google Scholar and journals
- Automatically retrieve paper metadata

### 🧠 Research Organization
- Tag and categorize research papers
- Manage research projects efficiently

---

## ⭐ 3. Unique Features of Paperpile AI

### 🔹 Google Docs Integration
Allows users to insert citations directly into Google Docs.

### 🔹 Automatic Metadata Extraction
Automatically extracts title, author, and publication details.

### 🔹 Cloud-Based Research Library
Store and access research papers online.

### 🔹 Academic Workflow Support
Designed specifically for academic writing and research management.
