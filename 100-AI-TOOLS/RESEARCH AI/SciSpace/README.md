# 📄 SciSpace — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is SciSpace?

SciSpace is an AI-powered platform designed to help users read, understand, and analyze research papers more easily.

It provides tools like **SciSpace Copilot**, which explains difficult sections of academic papers and summarizes complex research.

SciSpace is widely used by students, researchers, and academics to simplify scientific literature.

---

## 🎯 2. What Can SciSpace Do? (Core Functions)

### 📄 Research Paper Explanation
- Explain complex research papers
- Simplify technical language
- Help understand scientific terms

### 📊 Paper Summarization
- Generate summaries of academic papers
- Extract key insights and findings

### 🔎 Research Paper Search
- Discover academic papers
- Explore research topics

### 🧠 AI Research Assistant
- Ask questions about a research paper
- Get explanations of formulas, graphs, and tables

## ⭐ 3. Unique Features of SciSpace

### 🔹 SciSpace Copilot
An AI assistant that explains research papers line by line.

### 🔹 PDF Paper Analysis
Users can upload research papers and ask questions about them.

### 🔹 Citation and Reference Tools
Helps manage citations and references.
