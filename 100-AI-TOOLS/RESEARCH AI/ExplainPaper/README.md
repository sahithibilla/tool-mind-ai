# 📑 Explainpaper — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Explainpaper?

Explainpaper is an AI-powered tool designed to help users understand complex academic research papers.

Users can upload a PDF of a research paper and highlight difficult sections, and the AI will explain them in simple language.

Explainpaper is widely used by students, researchers, and developers who want to understand technical papers more easily.

## 🎯 2. What Can Explainpaper Do? (Core Functions)

### 📄 Research Paper Explanation
- Explain difficult sections of research papers

- Help understand technical concepts

### 📑 PDF Analysis
- Upload research papers in PDF format
- Highlight specific sentences or paragraphs

### 🧠 Concept Simplification
