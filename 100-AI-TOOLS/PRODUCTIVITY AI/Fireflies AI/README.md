# 🔥 Fireflies AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Fireflies AI?

Fireflies AI is an AI-powered meeting assistant that records, transcribes, and summarizes meetings automatically.

It integrates with platforms like Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams to capture meeting conversations and generate notes.

Fireflies AI is widely used by teams, managers, and professionals to track discussions and avoid missing important information.

---

## 🎯 2. What Can Fireflies AI Do? (Core Functions)

### 🎙️ Meeting Transcription
- Automatically transcribe meetings
- Convert speech into text
- Save meeting conversations

### 📝 AI Meeting Summaries
- Generate summaries of meetings
- Highlight key decisions and action items

### 🔎 Meeting Search
- Search meeting transcripts
- Find specific discussions quickly

### 👥 Collaboration Tools
- Share meeting notes with teams
- Assign action items from discussions

## ⭐ 3. Unique Features of Fireflies AI

### 🔹 Automatic Meeting Recording
Fireflies can join meetings and record them automatically.

### 🔹 AI Meeting Summaries
Creates short summaries of long meetings.

### 🔹 Conversation Intelligence
Analyzes discussions and extracts important points.

### 🔹 Integration with Collaboration Tools
Works with Slack, Notion, CRM systems, and project management tools.
