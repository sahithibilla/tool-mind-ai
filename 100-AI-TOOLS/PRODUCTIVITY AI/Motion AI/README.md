# ⏱️ Motion AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Motion AI?

Motion AI is an AI-powered productivity and scheduling tool that automatically plans your tasks and meetings.

It combines task management, calendar scheduling, and AI automation to help users manage their time more efficiently.

Motion AI is widely used by professionals, entrepreneurs, and teams who want to automate their daily schedules.
