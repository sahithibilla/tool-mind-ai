# 🔁 Rewind AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Rewind AI?

Rewind AI is an AI-powered personal memory tool that records and indexes everything you see, say, or hear on your computer.

It allows users to search through past meetings, webpages, documents, and conversations instantly.

Rewind AI is often described as a **"search engine for your life on your computer."**

It is widely used by professionals, researchers, and knowledge workers who want to recall information quickly.

---

## 🎯 2. What Can Rewind AI Do? (Core Functions)

### 🔎 Personal Memory Search
- Search everything you've seen on your computer
- Find past documents, webpages, and conversations

### 🎥 Screen Recording
- Automatically records screen activity
- Allows users to review past actions

### 🎙️ Meeting Recording
- Capture meeting conversations
- Transcribe discussions

### 🧠 AI Knowledge Retrieval
- Ask questions about past work
- Retrieve information instantly
