# ⚡ Taskade AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Taskade AI?

Taskade AI is an AI-powered productivity and collaboration platform that helps individuals and teams manage tasks, projects, and workflows.

It combines task management, note-taking, mind mapping, and AI assistance into a single workspace.

Taskade AI is widely used by teams, entrepreneurs, and professionals to plan projects, automate workflows, and organize work efficiently.
