# 📒 Reflect AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Reflect AI?

Reflect AI is a minimalist, AI-powered note-taking and journaling application.  
It focuses on **privacy, speed, and networked thought**, helping users capture ideas, link them together, and build a personal knowledge base.  

Reflect integrates **GPT-4 for writing assistance**, **Whisper for voice transcription**, and **end-to-end encryption** for secure note storage.  

---

## 🎯 2. What Can Reflect AI Do? (Core Functions)

### 📝 AI Note-Taking
- Generate and rewrite notes using GPT-4  
- Create structured, linked entries  
- Support journaling and meeting notes  

### 🎙️ Voice Transcription
- Convert speech to text with Whisper AI  
- Capture meetings, lectures, or personal thoughts  

### 🔗 Networked Notes
- Build a “second brain” with backlinks  
- Connect ideas across entries automatically  

### 📅 Productivity Integration
- Calendar sync for meeting notes  
- Instant device sync  
- Offline mode support
