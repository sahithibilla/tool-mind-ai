# 📧 Superhuman AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Superhuman AI?

Superhuman AI is a premium email client that integrates artificial intelligence to streamline email workflows.  
It helps users **write faster, manage inboxes efficiently, and automate repetitive tasks** while maintaining a sleek, productivity-focused interface.  

Created by **Rahul Vohra**, Superhuman is known for its speed, keyboard shortcuts, and AI-powered executive assistant features.  

---

## 🎯 2. What Can Superhuman AI Do? (Core Functions)

### ✍️ AI Writing Assistance
- Draft replies automatically  
- Rewrite emails in your tone  
- Summarize long threads  

### 📥 Inbox Management
- Smart triage with Split Inbox  
- Auto-labeling and auto-archiving  
- Unsubscribe suggestions  

### ⏰ Productivity Boost
- Auto reminders for follow-ups  
- Read receipts and insights  
- Calendar integration  

### 🤖 AI Automation
- “Ask AI” for quick answers  
- Auto-drafts for repetitive emails  
- Suggested replies
