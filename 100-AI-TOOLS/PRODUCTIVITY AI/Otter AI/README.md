# 🦦 Otter AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Otter AI?

Otter AI is an AI-powered transcription and meeting assistant that converts spoken conversations into text in real time.

It is widely used for meetings, lectures, interviews, and voice notes. Otter can automatically generate meeting transcripts, summaries, and searchable notes.

Otter AI integrates with platforms like Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams to capture meeting discussions.

---

## 🎯 2. What Can Otter AI Do? (Core Functions)

### 🎙️ Live Transcription
- Convert speech into text in real time
- Record meetings and lectures
- Generate automatic transcripts

### 📝 AI Meeting Notes
- Generate summaries of conversations
- Highlight key discussion points
- Identify action items

### 🔎 Searchable Transcripts
- Search words inside meeting recordings
- Jump to specific moments in audio

### 👥 Collaboration
- Share transcripts with team members
- Add comments and highlights
