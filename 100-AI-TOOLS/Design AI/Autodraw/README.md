# ✏️ AutoDraw — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is AutoDraw?

AutoDraw is a **free AI-powered drawing tool** created by Google’s Creative Lab.  
It helps users turn rough sketches into polished illustrations by predicting what they’re trying to draw and offering suggestions.  

It’s designed for **non-artists, educators, and casual creators** who want quick visuals without needing advanced design skills.

---

## 🎯 2. What Can AutoDraw Do? (Core Functions)

### 🖌️ Smart Drawing
- Recognizes rough sketches and suggests polished icons  
- Converts doodles into clean illustrations instantly  

### 🎨 Illustration Library
- Offers thousands of ready-made icons and drawings  
- Covers categories like animals, food, objects, and symbols  

### 📐 Simple Editing
- Resize, rotate, and recolor illustrations  
- Combine multiple icons into a single design  

### 📤 Export & Share
- Download drawings as PNG files  
- Share directly online
