# 🖌️ Galileo AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Galileo AI?

Galileo AI is an **AI-powered design platform** that generates **user interface (UI) mockups and prototypes** directly from text prompts.  
It allows product managers, startups, and developers to **turn ideas into polished, editable designs instantly**, exportable to Figma for refinement.

---

## 🎯 2. What Can Galileo AI Do? (Core Functions)

### ✍️ Text-to-UI Generation
- Describe your app or website idea in plain language  
- Galileo produces ready-to-use mockups  

### 🎨 Autodesigner
- Generates full dashboards, landing pages, or app screens from prompts  
- Provides multiple design variations  

### 📐 Editable Designs
- Customize fonts, layouts, and components  
- Adjust generated mockups to match brand identity  

### 📤 Figma Export
- Export designs directly into Figma  
- Continue editing with professional design tools  

### 🛠️ Collaboration
- Share prototypes with teams and stakeholders  
- Speed up brainstorming and iteration
