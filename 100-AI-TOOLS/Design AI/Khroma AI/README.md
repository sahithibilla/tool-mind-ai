# 🎨 Khroma AI — Complete Guide

## 🧠 1. What is Khroma AI?

Khroma AI is an **AI-powered color palette generator** that learns your preferences and creates personalized color combinations.  
It uses a **neural network trained on your favorite colors** to generate palettes, gradients, and typography samples tailored to your style.  

Khroma is widely used by **designers, developers, and brand creators** to build cohesive and accessible color schemes.

---

## 🎯 2. What Can Khroma AI Do? (Core Functions)

### 🎨 Personalized Palette Generation
- Train Khroma by selecting colors you like  
- Blocks colors you dislike  
- Generates infinite palettes based on your taste  

### 🖌️ Gradient & Typography Samples
- Creates gradients and text samples with chosen palettes  
- Helps visualize colors in real-world design contexts  

### 🔍 Search & Filter
- Find colors by hue, tint, value, hex, or RGB  
- Explore thousands of human-made palettes  

### 📊 Accessibility Checks
- Provides WCAG contrast scores  
- Ensures readability and inclusivity in design  

### 💾 Save & Export
- Save favorite palettes to your library  
- Export hex codes, RGB values, and CSS snippets  

---

## ⭐ 3. Unique Features of Khroma AI

- **Learns your personal style** instead of offering generic palettes  
- **Infinite combinations** generated by AI  
- **Accessibility scoring** for inclusive design  
- **Browser-based tool** (no installation required)
